What you will find

Tales are added over time. Some are light as pipe-smoke; others are darker, older, and stranger — the sort of thing you hear once and remember on a long road.

  • Short tales and vignettes
  • Folktales (the kind that change with each telling)
  • Songs, riddles, and verse
  • Fragments and drafts that may be polished
